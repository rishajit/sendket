Music Influences

The Velvet Underground

The Velvet Underground had a profound influence on me growing up. Their experimental and alternative music challenged my musical tastes and broadened my horizons, sparking my passion for unique and unconventional sounds.

Nirvana

Nirvana's raw and powerful music resonated deeply with me during my formative years. Their authentic and rebellious spirit inspired me to explore my own creativity and expressiveness through music.

Jeff Buckley

Jeff Buckley's hauntingly beautiful voice and poetic lyrics left an indelible mark on my soul. His emotional depth and vulnerability in his music taught me the power of conveying profound emotions through songwriting and performance.
